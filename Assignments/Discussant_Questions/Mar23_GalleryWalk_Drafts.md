## 1. Piter's Discussion Questions for the Document
*(You can copy and paste these into the group's shared Google Doc)*

**Question 1 (Desmond & Fine): Constraints & Knowledge** 
How do neoliberal reforms and historically produced resource deprivation reshape what counts as "knowledge" in schools? When schools face extreme austerity, how can we hold structural inequality accountable rather than blaming students for "failing" standard tests? 

**Question 2 (Talusan): Everyday Sorting & Habits**
Talusan discusses habits for noticing class-based exclusion. What are some everyday classroom routines, “neutral” policies, or technological tools that quietly sort students by class status? How can educators use identity-conscious practice to interrupt this invisible sorting?
