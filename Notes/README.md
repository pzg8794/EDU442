## How to Use These Notes

**Before class:**
1. Read the week's summary in [EDU442-Notes.md](EDU442-Notes.md#4-readings-schedule--quick-study-summaries)
2. Use "what to skim" guidance to focus reading time
3. Prepare your synthesis and questions using the **3.1 Weekly workflow** section

**During class:**
1. Take notes in class-notes.txt or on paper
2. Capture key quotes and discussion points

**After class:**
1. Integrate class notes into EDU442-Notes.md (main document)
2. Develop your claim statements and concrete moves
3. Connect to your PhD strategy (PhDStrategy-ReadingReview-and-ToDo.md if applicable)

## Study System

The notes use a **reusable weekly workflow** (see [EDU442-Notes.md Section 3.1](EDU442-Notes.md#31-weekly-workflow-minimum-viable)):

For each week, produce:
1. **Synthesis:** 6-10 sentences across readings
2. **3 transferable claims:** Reusable for EQUITAS / PhD writing / Impacting Practice
3. **EQUITAS lens:** 1 paragraph on what this changes about measurement/evaluation
4. **2 concrete moves:** Classroom/policy/tool actions you can take

This approach ensures notes are actionable, not just decorative.
